DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE

Master of Science (Computer Science) is a 2 -year full time programme designed to develop student’s knowledge and expertise in Computer Science and be ready for industry placement. The M.Sc programme in Computer Science is designed to provide students an overview of computing, an understanding of the concepts, principles and skills in their application and extension, and a practical experience in applied computing. The programme emphasizes the fundamental principles underlying computing and helps develop an understanding of the computer science considerations involved in computing system design, implementation and usage.



SALIENT FEATURES OF DEPARTMENT

  • Qualified and Experienced team of faculty members with wider experience in subject.
  • Career opportunity seminars.
  • Regular counseling/mentoring for students.
  • Campus recruitments of students in Reputed IT industries.


OBJECTIVES OF DEPARTMENT

  • To provide quality education in Computer Science.
  • To inspire and motivate faculty as well as students for excellent teamwork.
  • To adopt progressively new technologies in teaching and learning process.
  • To teach students lifelong learning skills, which will allows them to successfully adopt to evolving technologies throughout their professional careers.
