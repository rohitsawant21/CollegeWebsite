DEPARTMENT OF ZOOLOGY

The Zoology department established from 1982.



SALIENT FEATURES OF DEPARTMENT

  • Department of Zoology, Shivraj College, Gadhinglaj established in the year 1982 as a part of Science stream.
  • This department offer under graduate such as B.Sc I and B.Sc II (Zoology).


OBJECTIVES OF DEPARTMENT

  • Field visit and study tour- To various places to study and observe form in natural habitat.
  • Demonstration of videos based on syllabus.
  • Preparation of study material -
    • B.Sc I Practical hand book.
    • B.Sc II Practical hand book.
