DEPARTMENT OF ENGLISH

Department of English has been established in year 1964.It provides specialized knowledge and training in British literature. New literature's in English linguistics, ELT, comparative literature,English translation studies and communicative competence.



SALIENT FEATURES OF DEPARTMENT

  • Well qualified and experienced teaching faculty.
  • Well equipped and Digital language lab.
  • Digital learning resources.
  • Advanced teaching and learning process.
  • Spoken English classes and Remedial coaching for students.
  • Workshops,Guest lectures,Seminars,Exhibition and Quiz competition for overall development of the students


OBJECTIVES OF DEPARTMENT

  • To motivate students to improve their communication skills.
  • To develop skills, confidents and competence in English.
  • To provide the highest quality education in English literature and Linguistics.
  • To enhance the knowledge of English language and literature.
